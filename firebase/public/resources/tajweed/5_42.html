Listeners

to [the] falsehood,

devourers

of the forbidden.

So if

they come to you

then judge

between them

or

turn away

from them.

And if

you turn away

from them,

then never

will they harm you

(in) anything.

And if

you judge,

then judge

between them

with [the] justice.

Indeed,

Allah

loves

the ones who are just.
