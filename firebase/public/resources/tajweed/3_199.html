And indeed,

among

(the) People

(of) the Book

(are those) who

believe

in Allah

and what

was revealed

to you

and what

was revealed

to them -

humbly submissive

to Allah.

Not

(do) they exchange

[with] (the) Verses

(of) Allah

(for) a price

little.

Those,

for them,

their reward

(is) with

their Lord.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) swift

(in taking) the account.
