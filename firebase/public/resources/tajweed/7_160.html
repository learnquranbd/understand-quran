And We divided them

(into) two

(and) ten [i.e. twelve]

tribes

(as) communities.

And We inspired

to

Musa,

when

asked him for water

his people,

[that]

"Strike

with your staff

the stone."

Then gushed forth

from it

two

(and) ten [i.e. twelve]

springs.

Certainly,

knew

each

people

their drinking place.

And We shaded

[on] them

(with) the clouds

and We sent down

upon them,

the manna

and the quails.

"Eat

from

(the) good things

which

We have provided you."

And not

they wronged Us

but

they were

(to) themselves

doing wrong.
