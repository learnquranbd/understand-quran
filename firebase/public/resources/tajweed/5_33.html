Only

(the) recompense

(for) those who

wage war

(against) Allah

and His Messenger

and strive

in

the earth

spreading corruption

(is) that

they be killed

or

they be crucified

or

be cut off

their hands

and their feet

of

opposite sides

or

they be exiled

from

the land.

That

(is) for them

disgrace

in

the world

and for them

in

the Hereafter

(is) a punishment

great.
