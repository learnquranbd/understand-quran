O you

who

believe[d]!

(Do) not

render in vain

your charities

with reminders (of it)

or [the] hurt,

like the one who

spends

his wealth

(to) be seen

(by) the people,

and (does) not

believe

in Allah

and the Day

[the] Last.

Then his example

(is) like

(that of a) smooth rock

upon it

(is) dust,

then fell on it

heavy rain,

then left it

bare.

Not

they have control

on

anything

of what

they (have) earned.

And Allah

(does) not

guide

the people

[the] disbelieving.
