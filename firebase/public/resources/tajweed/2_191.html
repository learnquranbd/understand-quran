And kill them

wherever

you find them,

and drive them out

from

wherever

they drove you out,

and [the] oppression

(is) worse

than

[the] killing.

And (do) not

fight them

near

Al-Masjid

Al-Haraam

until

they fight you

in it.

Then if

they fight you,

then kill them.

Such

(is the) reward

(of) the disbelievers.
