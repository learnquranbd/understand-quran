Say,

"Only

(had) forbidden

my Lord

the shameful deeds

what

(is) apparent

of it

and what

is concealed,

and the sin,

and the oppression

without

[the] right,

and that

you associate (others)

with Allah

what

not

He (has) sent down

of it

any authority,

and that

you say

about

Allah

what

not

you know."
