O

Messenger!

Let not

grieve you

those who

hasten

in (to)

[the] disbelief -

of

those who

said,

"We believe"

with their mouths

and not

believe

their hearts,

and from

those who

(are) Jews.

They (are) listeners

to falsehood,

(and) listeners

for people,

other,

(who have) not

come to you.

They distort

the words

from

after

their context,

saying,

"If

you are given

this

[so] take it

but if

not

you are given it

then beware."

And (for) whom

intends

Allah

his trial,

then never

will you have power

for him

against

Allah

anything.

Those

(are) the ones

never

will intend

Allah

that

He purifies

their hearts.

For them

in

the world

(is) disgrace

and for them

in

the Hereafter

(is) a punishment

great.
