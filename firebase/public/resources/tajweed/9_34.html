O you who believe!

O you who believe!

O you who believe!

Indeed,

many

of

the rabbis

and the monks

surely eat

(the) wealth

(of) the people

in falsehood,

and hinder

from

(the) way

(of) Allah.

And those who

hoard

the gold

and the silver,

and (do) not

spend it

in

(the) way

(of) Allah,

[so] give them tidings

of a punishment

painful.
