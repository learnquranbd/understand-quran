Say,

"We have believed

in Allah

and what

(is) revealed

to us

and what

was revealed

to

Ibrahim

and Ishmael

and Isaac

and Yaqub

and the descendants,

and what

was given

(to) Musa

and Isa

and what

was given

(to) the Prophets

from

their Lord.

Not

we make distinction

between

any

of them.

And we

to Him

(are) submissive."
