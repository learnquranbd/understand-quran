O you

who

believe!

Whoever

turns back

among you

from

his religion,

then soon

(will be) brought

(by) Allah

a people

whom He loves

and they love Him,

humble

towards

the believers

(and) stern

towards

the disbelievers;

striving

in

(the) way

(of) Allah

and not

fearing

the blame

(of) a critic.

That

(is the) Grace

(of) Allah,

He grants

whom

He wills.

And Allah

(is) All-Encompassing,

All-Knowing.
