Or

you say,

"If

[that]

was revealed

to us

the Book

surely we (would) have been

better guided

than them.

So verily

has come to you

clear proofs

from

your Lord

and a Guidance

and a Mercy.

Then who

(is) more unjust

than (he) who

denies

[with] (the) Verses

(of) Allah,

and turns away

from them?

We will recompense

those who

turn away

from

Our Signs

(with) an evil

punishment

because

they used to

turn away.
