Then because of

their breaking

(of) their covenant

and their disbelief

in (the) Signs

(of) Allah

and their killing

(of) the Prophets

without

any right

and their saying,

"Our hearts

(are) wrapped."

Nay,

(has) set a seal

Allah

on their (hearts)

for their disbelief

so not

they believe

except

a few.
