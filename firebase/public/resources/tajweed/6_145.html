Say,

"Not

(do) I find

in

what

has been revealed

to me

(anything) forbidden

to

an eater

who eats it

except

that

it be

dead

or

blood

poured forth

or

(the) flesh

(of) swine -

for indeed, it

(is) filth -

or

(it be) disobedience,

[is] dedicated

to other than

Allah

[on it].

But whoever

(is) compelled

not

desiring

and not

transgressing,

then indeed,

your Lord

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful."
