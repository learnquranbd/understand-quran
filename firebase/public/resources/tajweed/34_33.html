And will say

those who

were oppressed

to those who

were arrogant,

"Nay,

(it was) a plot

(by) night

and (by) day

when

you were ordering us

that

we disbelieve

in Allah

and we set up

for Him

equals."

But they will conceal

the regret

when

they see

the punishment.

And We will put

shackles

on

(the) necks

(of) those who

disbelieved.

Will

they be recompensed

except

(for) what

they used to

do?
