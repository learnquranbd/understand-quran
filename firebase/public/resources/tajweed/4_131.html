And for Allah

(is) whatever

(is) in

the heavens

and whatever

(is) in

the earth.

And surely

We have instructed

those who

were given

the Book

from

before you

and yourselves

that

you fear

Allah.

But if

you disbelieve -

then indeed

for Allah

(is) whatever

(is) in

the heavens

and whatever

(is) in

the earth.

And is

Allah

Free of need,

Praiseworthy.
