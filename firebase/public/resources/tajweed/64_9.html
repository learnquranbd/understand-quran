(The) Day

He will assemble you

for (the) Day

(of) the Assembly,

that

(will be the) Day

(of) mutual loss and gain.

And whoever

believes

in Allah

and does

righteous deeds

He will remove

from him

his evil deeds

and He will admit him

(to) Gardens

flow

from

underneath it

the rivers,

abiding

therein

forever.

That

(is) the success

the great.
