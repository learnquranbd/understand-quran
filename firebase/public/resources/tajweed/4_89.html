They wish

if

you disbelieve

as

they disbelieved

and you would be

alike.

So (do) not

take

from them

allies

until

they emigrate

in

(the) way

(of) Allah.

But if

they turn back,

seize them

and kill them

wherever

you find them.

And (do) not

take

from them

any ally

and not
