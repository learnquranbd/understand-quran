And to

those who

are Jews

We forbade

every

(animal) with

claws,

and of

the cows

and the sheep

We forbade

to them

their fat

except

what

carried

their backs

or

the entrails

or

what

(is) joined

with the bone.

That

(is) their recompense

for their rebellion.

And indeed, We

[surely] are truthful.
