(There is) no

compulsion

in

the religion.

Surely

has become distinct

the right (path)

from

the wrong.

Then whoever

disbelieves

in false deities

and believes

in Allah,

then surely

he grasped

the handhold -

[the] firm,

(which) not

(will) break

[for it].

And Allah

(is) All-Hearing,

All-Knowing.
