And of

the people

(is he) who

says,

"We believe

in Allah."

But when

he is harmed

in

(the Way of) Allah

he considers

(the) trial

(of) the people

as (the) punishment

(of) Allah.

But if

comes

victory

from

your Lord,

surely they say,

"Indeed, we

were

with you."

Is not

Allah

most knowing

of what

(is) in

(the) breasts

(of) the worlds?
