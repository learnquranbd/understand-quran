And He

(is) the One Who

produced

gardens

trellised

and other than

trellised

and the date-palm

and the crops,

diverse

(are) its taste,

and the olives

and the pomegranates

similar

and other than

similar.

Eat

of

its fruit

when

it bears fruit,

and give

its due

(on the) day

(of) its harvest.

And (do) not

(be) extravagant.

Indeed, He

(does) not

love

the ones who are extravagant.
