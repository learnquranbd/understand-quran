They said,

"O Lut!

Indeed, we

(are) messengers

(of) your Lord,

never

they will reach

you.

So travel

with your family

in a part

of

the night

and (let) not

look back

anyone of you,

anyone of you,

except

your wife.

Indeed, it

will strike her

what

will strike them.

Indeed,

their appointed time

(is) morning.

Is not

the morning

near?"
