And if

that was

any Quran,

could be moved

by it

the mountains,

or

could be cloven asunder

by it

the earth,

or

could be made to speak

by it

the dead.

Nay,

with Allah

(is) the command

all.

Then do not

know

those who

believe

that

if

had willed

Allah,

surely, He would have guided

all?

all of the mankind?

And not

will cease

those who

disbelieve

to strike them

for what

they did

a disaster,

or

it settles

close

from

their homes

until

comes

(the) promise

(of) Allah.

Indeed,

Allah

(will) not

fail

(in) the Promise.
