O you

who

believe[d]!

Believe

in Allah

and His Messenger,

and the Book

which

He revealed

upon

His Messenger

and the Book

which

He revealed

from

before.

And whoever

disbelieves

in Allah

and His Angels,

and His Books,

and His Messengers

and the Day

the Last,

then surely

he (has) lost (the) way,

straying

far away.
