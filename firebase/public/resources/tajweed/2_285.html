Believed

the Messenger

in what

was revealed

to him

from

his Lord

and the believers.

All

believed

in Allah,

and His Angels,

and His Books,

and His Messengers.

"Not

we make distinction

between

any

of

His messengers."

And they said,

"We heard

and we obeyed.

(Grant) us Your forgiveness

our Lord,

and to You

(is) the return."
