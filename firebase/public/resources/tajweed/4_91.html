You will find

others

wishing

that

they be secure from you

and they be secure from

their people,

Everytime

that

they are returned

to

the temptation,

they are plunged

into it.

So if

not

they withdraw from you

and offer

to you

[the] peace

and they restrain

their hands,

then seize them

and kill them

wherever

you find them.

And those -

We made

for you

against them

an authority

clear.
