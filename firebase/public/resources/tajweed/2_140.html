Or

(do) you say

that

Ibrahim

and Ishmael

and Isaac

and Yaqub

and the descendants

were

Jews

or

Christians?"

Say,

"Are you

better knowing

or

(is) Allah?"

And who

(is) more unjust

than (the one) who

concealed

a testimony

(that) he has

from

Allah?

And not

(is) Allah

unaware

of what

you do.
