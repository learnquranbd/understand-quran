Surely

it was

for you

a sign

in

(the) two hosts

which met -

one group

fighting

in

(the) way

(of) Allah

and another

disbelievers.

They were seeing them

twice of them

with the sight

(of) their eyes.

And Allah

supports

with His help

whom

He wills.

Indeed,

in

that

surely (is) a lesson

for the owners

(of) vision.
