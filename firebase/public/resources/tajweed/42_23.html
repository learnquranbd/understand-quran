That

(is of) which

Allah gives glad tidings

Allah gives glad tidings

(to) His slaves -

those who

believe

and do

righteous deeds.

Say,

"Not

I ask you

for it

any payment

except

the love

among

the relatives."

And whoever

earns

any good,

We increase

for him

therein

good.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

All-Appreciative.
