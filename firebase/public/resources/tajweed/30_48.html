Allah

(is) the One Who

sends

the winds,

so they raise

(the) clouds,

then He spreads them

in

the sky,

how

He wills,

and He makes them

fragments

so you see

the rain

coming forth

from

their midst.

Then when

He causes it to fall on

He causes it to fall on

whom

He wills

of

His slaves,

behold!

They

rejoice.
