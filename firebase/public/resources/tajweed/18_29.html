And say,

"The truth

(is) from

your Lord,

so whoever

wills -

let him believe

and whoever

wills -

let him disbelieve."

Indeed, We

have prepared

for the wrongdoers

a Fire,

will surround

them

its walls.

And if

they call for relief,

they will be relieved

with water

like molten brass,

(which) scalds

the faces.

Wretched

(is) the drink,

and evil

(is) the resting place.
