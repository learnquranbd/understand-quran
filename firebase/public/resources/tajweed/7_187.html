They ask you

about

the Hour,

when will be

its appointed time?

Say,

"Only

its knowledge

(is) with

my Lord,

no (one)

can reveal [it]

its time

except

Him.

It lays heavily

in

the heavens

and the earth.

Not

will it come to you

but

suddenly."

They ask you

as if you

(were) well informed

about it.

Say,

"Only

its knowledge

(is) with

Allah,

but

most

(of) the people

(do) not

know."
