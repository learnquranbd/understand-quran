Those who

follow

the Messenger,

the Prophet,

the unlettered,

whom

they find him

written

with them

in

the Taurat

and the Injeel.

He commands them

to the right

and forbids them

from

the wrong,

and he makes lawful

for them

the pure things

and makes unlawful

for them

the impure things

and he relieves

from them

their burden

and the fetters

which

were

upon them.

So those who

believe

in him

and honor him,

and help him

and follow

the light

which

has been sent down

with him -

Those (are)

[they]

the successful ones."
