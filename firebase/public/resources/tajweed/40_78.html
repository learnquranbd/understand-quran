And certainly

We have sent

Messengers

before you.

before you.

Among them

(are) who -

We have related

to you,

and among them

(are) who -

not

We have related

to you.

And not

is

for any Messenger

that

he brings

a Sign

except

by (the) permission

(of) Allah.

So when

comes

(the) Command

(of) Allah,

it will be decided

in truth,

and will lose

there

the falsifiers.
