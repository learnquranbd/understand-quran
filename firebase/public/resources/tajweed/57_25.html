Certainly

We sent

Our Messengers

with clear proofs

and We sent down

with them

the Scripture

and the Balance

that may establish

the people

justice.

And We sent down

[the] iron,

wherein

(is) power

mighty

and benefits

for the people,

and so that Allah may make evident

and so that Allah may make evident

(he) who

helps Him

and His Messengers,

unseen.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) All-Strong

All-Mighty.
