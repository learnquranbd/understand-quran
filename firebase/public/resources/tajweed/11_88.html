He said,

"O my people!

Do you see

if

I am

on

a clear evidence

from

my Lord,

and He has provided me

from Himself

a good provision?

a good provision?

And not

I intend

that

I differ from you

in

what

I forbid you

from it.

Not

I intend

except

the reform

as much as I am able.

as much as I am able.

And not

(is) my success

except

with Allah.

Upon Him

I trust

and to Him

I turn.
