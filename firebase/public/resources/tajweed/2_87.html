And indeed

We gave

Musa

the Book

and We followed up

from

after him

with [the] Messengers.

And We gave

Isa,

(the) son

(of) Maryam,

[the] clear signs

and We supported him

(with)

the Holy Spirit.

Is it (not) so (that) whenever

came to you

a Messenger

with what

(does) not

desire

yourselves,

you acted arrogantly?

So a party

you denied,

and a party

you kill(ed).
