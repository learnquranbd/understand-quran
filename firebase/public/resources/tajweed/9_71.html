And the believing men

and the believing women,

some of them

(are) allies

(of) others.

They enjoin

the right,

and forbid

from

the wrong,

and they establish

the prayer

and give

the zakah,

and they obey

Allah

and His Messenger.

Those,

Allah will have mercy on them.

Allah will have mercy on them.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) All-Mighty,

All-Wise.
