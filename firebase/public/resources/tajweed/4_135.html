O you

who

believe[d]!

Be

custodians

of justice

(as) witnesses

to Allah,

even if

(it is) against

yourselves

or

the parents

and the relatives.

if

he be

rich

or

poor,

for Allah

(is) nearer

to both of them.

So (do) not

follow

the desire

lest

you deviate.

And if

you distort

or

refrain,

then indeed,

Allah

is

of what

you do

All-Aware.
