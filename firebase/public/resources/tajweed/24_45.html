And Allah

created

every

moving creature

from

water.

Of them

(is a kind) who

walks

on

its belly,

and of them

(is a kind) who

walks

on

two legs,

and of them

(is a kind) who

walks

on

four.

Allah creates

Allah creates

what

He wills.

Indeed,

Allah

on

every

thing

(is) All-Powerful.
