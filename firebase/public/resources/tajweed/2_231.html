And when

you divorce

the women

and they reach

their (waiting) term,

then retain them

in a fair manner

or

release them

in a fair manner.

And (do) not

retain them

(to) hurt

so that you transgress.

And whoever

does

that,

then indeed,

he wronged

himself.

And (do) not

take

(the) Verses

(of) Allah

(in) jest,

and remember

(the) Favors

(of) Allah

upon you

and what

(is) revealed

to you

of

the Book

and [the] wisdom;

He instructs you

with it.

And fear

Allah

and know

that

Allah (is)

of every

thing

All-Knower.
