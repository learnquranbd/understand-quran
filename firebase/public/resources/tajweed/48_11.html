Will say

to you

those who remained behind

of

the Bedouins,

"Kept us busy

our properties

and our families,

so ask forgiveness

for us."

They say

with their tongues

what

is not

in

their hearts.

Say,

"Then who

has power

for you

against

Allah

(in) anything,

if

He intends

for you

harm

or

He intends

for you

a benefit?

Nay,

is

Allah

of what

you do

All-Aware.
