And to

Thamud

(We sent) their brother

Salih.

He said,

"O my people!

Worship

Allah,

not

you have

any

god

other than Him.

He

produced you

from

the earth

and settled you

in it.

So ask forgiveness of Him,

then

turn in repentance

to Him.

Indeed,

my Lord

(is) near,

All-Responsive."
