Only

the charities

(are) for the poor,

and the needy

and those who collect

them,

and the ones inclined

their hearts,

and in

the (freeing of) the necks,

and for those in debt

and in

(the) way

(of) Allah,

and the wayfarer -

and the wayfarer -

an obligation

from

Allah.

And Allah

(is) All-Knowing,

All-Wise.
