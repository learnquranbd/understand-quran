Evil (is) that

(for) which they have sold

with

themselves,

that

they disbelieve

in what

has revealed

Allah,

grudging

that

sends down

Allah

of

His Grace

on

whom

He wills

from

His servants.

So they have drawn (on themselves)

wrath

upon

wrath.

And for the disbelievers

(is) a punishment

humiliating.
