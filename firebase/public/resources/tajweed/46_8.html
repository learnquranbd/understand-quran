Or

they say,

"He has invented it."

Say,

"If

I have invented it

then not

you have power

for me

against

Allah

anything.

He

knows best

of what

you utter

concerning it.

Sufficient is He

Sufficient is He

(as) a Witness

between me

and between you,

and He

(is) the Oft-Forgiving,

the Most Merciful.
