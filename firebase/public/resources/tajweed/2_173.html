Only

He has forbidden

to you

the dead animals,

and [the] blood,

and flesh,

(of) swine,

and what

has been dedicated

[with it]

to other than

Allah.

So whoever

(is) forced by necessity

without

(being) disobedient

and not

transgressor,

then no

sin

on him.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
