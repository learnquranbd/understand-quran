O you

who

believe[d]!

Not

(is) lawful

for you

that

you inherit

the women

(by) force.

And not

you constraint them

so that you may take

a part

(of) what

you have given them

except

that

they commit

immorality

open.

And live with them

in kindness.

But if

you dislike them,

then perhaps

that

you dislike

a thing

and has placed

Allah

in it

good

much.
