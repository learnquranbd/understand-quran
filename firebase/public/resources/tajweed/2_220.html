Concerning

the world

and the Hereafter.

They ask you

about

the orphans.

Say,

"Setting right (their affairs)

for them

(is) best.

And if

you associate with them

then they (are) your brothers.

And Allah

knows

the corrupter

from

the amender.

And if

(had) willed

Allah

surely He (could have) put you in difficulties.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) All-Mighty,

All-Wise."
