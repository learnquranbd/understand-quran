And when

you divorce

[the] women

and they reached

their (waiting) term,

then (do) not

hinder them

[that]

(from) marrying

their husbands

when

they agree

between themselves

in a fair manner.

That

is admonished

with it

whoever

[is]

among you

believes

in Allah

and the Day

[the] Last;

that

(is) more virtuous

for you

and more purer.

And Allah

knows

and you

(do) not

know.
