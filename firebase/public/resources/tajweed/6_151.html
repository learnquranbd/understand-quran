Say,

"Come,

I will recite

what

has prohibited

your Lord

to you.

That (do) not

associate

with Him

anything,

and with the parents

(be) good,

and (do) not

kill

your children

(out) of

poverty,

We

provide for you

and for them.

And (do) not

go near

[the] immoralities

what

(is) apparent

of them

and what

(is) concealed.

And (do) not

kill

the soul

which

has (been) forbidden

(by) Allah

except

by (legal) right.

That

(He) has enjoined on you

with it,

so that you may

use reason."
