Those who

are waiting

for you.

Then if

was

for you

a victory

from

Allah

they say,

"Were not

we

with you?"

But if

(there) was

for the disbelievers

a chance

they said,

"Did not

we have advantage

over you

and we protected you

from

the believers?"

And Allah

will judge

between you

(on the) Day

(of) the Resurrection,

and never

will make

Allah

for the disbelievers

over

the believers

a way.
