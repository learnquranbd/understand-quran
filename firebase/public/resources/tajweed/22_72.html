are recited

to them

Our Verses

clear,

you will recognize

on

(the) faces

disbelieve

the denial.

attack

those who

recite

to them

Our Verses.

"Then shall I inform you

of worse

than

that?

The Fire,

Allah (has) promised it

Allah (has) promised it

(for) those who
