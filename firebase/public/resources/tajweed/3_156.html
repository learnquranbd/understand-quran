O you

who

believe[d]!

(Do) not

be

like those who

disbelieved

and they said

about their brothers

when

they traveled

in

the earth

or

they were

fighting,

"If

they had been

with us,

not

they (would have) died

and not

they (would have) been killed."

So makes

Allah

that

a regret

in

their hearts.

And Allah

gives life

and causes death,

and Allah

of what

you do

(is) All-Seer.
