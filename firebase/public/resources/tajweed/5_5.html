This day

are made lawful

for you

the good things;

and (the) food

(of) those who

were given

the Book

(is) lawful

for you,

and your food

(is) lawful

for them.

And the chaste women

from

the believers

and the chaste women

from

those who

were given

the Book

from

before you,

when

you have given them

their bridal due,

being chaste

not

being lewd

and not

ones (who are) taking

secret lovers.

And whoever

denies

the faith -

then surely

(are) wasted

his deeds

and he,

in

the Hereafter,

(will be) among

the losers.
