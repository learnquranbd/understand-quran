O you

who

believe[d]!

(Do) not

take

(as) intimates

from

other than yourselves,

not

they will spare you

(any) ruin.

They wish

what

distresses you.

Indeed,

(has become) apparent

the hatred

from

their mouths,

and what

conceals

their breasts

(is) greater.

Certainly

We made clear

for you

the Verses,

if

you were

(to use) reason.
