Certainly

disbelieved

those who

say,

"Indeed

Allah -

He

(is) the Messiah,

son

(of) Maryam."

While said

the Messiah,

"O Children

(of) Israel!

Worship

Allah,

my Lord

and your Lord."

Indeed, he

who

associates partners

with Allah,

then surely

(has) forbidden

Allah

for him

Paradise

and his abode

(will be) the Fire.

And not

for the wrongdoers

any

helpers.
