Only

He has forbidden

to you

the dead animal

and the blood

and the flesh

(of) the swine,

and what

has been dedicated

to other (than)

Allah

[with it].

But (if) one

(is) forced -

without (being)

disobedient,

and not

a transgressor -

then indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
