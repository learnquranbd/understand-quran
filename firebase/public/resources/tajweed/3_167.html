And that He (might) make evident

those who

(are) hypocrites.

And it was said

to them,

"Come,

fight

in

(the) way

(of) Allah

or

defend."

They said,

"If

we knew

fighting

certainly we (would have) followed you."

They -

to disbelief

that day

(were) nearer

than [them]

to the faith,

saying

with their mouths

what

was not

in

their hearts.

And Allah

(is) Most Knowing

of what

they conceal.
