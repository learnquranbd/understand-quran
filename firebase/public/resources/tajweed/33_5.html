Call them

by their fathers;

it

(is) more just

near

Allah.

But if

not

you know

their fathers -

then (they are) your brothers

in

[the] religion

and your friends.

But not is

upon you

any blame

in what

you made a mistake

in it,

but

what

intended

your hearts.

And Allah

And Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
