And when

are recited

to them

Our Verses

clear

they say,

"Not

(is) this

but

a man

who wishes

to

hinder you

from what

used

(to) worship

your forefathers."

And they say,

"Not

(is) this

except

a lie

invented."

And said

those who

disbelieved

about the truth

when

it came to them,

"Not

(is) this

except

a magic

obvious."
