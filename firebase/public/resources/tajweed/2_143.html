And thus

We made you

a community

(of the) middle way

so that you will be

witnesses

over

the mankind,

and will be

the Messenger

on you

a witness.

And not

We made

the direction of prayer

which

you were used to

[on it]

except

that We make evident

(he) who

follows

the Messenger

from (he) who

turns back

on

his heels.

And indeed,

it was

certainly a great (test)

except

for

those whom

guided

(by) Allah.

And not

will

Allah

let go waste

your faith.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) to [the] mankind

Full of Kindness,

Most Merciful.
