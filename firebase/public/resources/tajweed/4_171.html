O People

(of) the Book!

(Do) not

commit excess

in

your religion

and (do) not

say

about

Allah

except

the truth.

Only

the Messiah,

Isa,

son

(of) Maryam,

(was) a Messenger

(of) Allah

and His word

which He conveyed

to

Maryam

and a spirit

from Him.

So believe

in Allah

and His Messengers.

And (do) not

say,

"Three;"

desist

(it is) better

for you.

Only

Allah

(is) God

One.

Glory be to Him!

That

He (should) have

for Him

a son.

To Him (belongs)

whatever

(is) in

the heavens

and whatever

(is) in

the earth.

And is sufficient

Allah

(as) a Disposer of affairs.
