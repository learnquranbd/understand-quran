So when

she heard

of their scheming,

she sent

for them

and she prepared

for them

a banquet

and she gave

each

one

of them

a knife

and she said,

"Come out

before them."

Then when

they saw him

they greatly admired him,

and cut

their hands,

they said,

"Forbid

Allah,

not

(is) this

a man

not

(is) this

but

an angel

noble."
