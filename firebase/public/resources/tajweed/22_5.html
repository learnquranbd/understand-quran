O mankind!

O mankind!

If

you are

in

doubt

about

the Resurrection,

then indeed, We

We created you

from

dust,

then

from

a semen-drop

then

from

a clinging substance

then

from

an embryonic lump,

formed

and unformed,

and unformed,

that We may make clear

to you.

And We cause to remain

in

the wombs

what

We will

for

a term

appointed,

then

We bring you out

(as) a child,

[then]

that you may reach

[your] maturity.

And among you

(is he) who

dies,

and among you

(is he) who

is returned

to

the most abject

age,

so that not

he knows,

after

after

having known,

anything.

And you see

the earth

barren

then when

We send down

on it

water,

it gets stirred

and it swells

and grows

of

every

kind

beautiful.
