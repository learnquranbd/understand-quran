Do not

you see

that

Allah

sends down

from

the sky

water

and He makes it flow

(as) springs

in

the earth;

then

He produces

with it

crops

(of) different

colors;

then

they wither

and you see it

turn yellow;

then

He makes them

debris?

Indeed,

in

that

surely, (is) a reminder

for those of understanding.

for those of understanding.
