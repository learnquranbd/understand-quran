And when

comes to them

a matter

of

the security

or

[the] fear

they spread

[with] it.

But if

they (had) referred it

to

the Messenger

and to

those

(having) authority

among them,

surely would have known it

those who

draw correct conclusion (from) it

among them.

And if not

(had been the) bounty

(of) Allah

on you

and His Mercy,

surely you (would have) followed

the Shaitaan

except

a few.
