And the camels and cattle -

We have made them

for you

among

(the) Symbols

(of) Allah,

for you

therein

(is) good.

So mention

(the) name

(of) Allah

over them

(when) lined up;

and when

are down

their sides,

then eat

from them

and feed

the needy who do not ask

and the needy who ask.

Thus

We have subjected them

to you

so that you may

be grateful.
