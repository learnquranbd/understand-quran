O mankind!

O mankind!

Is set forth

an example,

so listen

to it.

Indeed,

those whom

you invoke

besides Allah

besides Allah

besides Allah

will never

create

a fly

even if

they gathered together

for it.

And if

snatched away from them

the fly

a thing

not

they (could) take it back

from it.

So weak

(are) the seeker

and the one who is sought.
