Say,

"Bring forward

your witnesses,

those who

testify

that

Allah

prohibited

this."

Then if

they testify

then (do) not

testify

with them.

And (do) not

follow

(the) desires

(of) those who

denied

Our Signs

and those who

(do) not

believe

in the Hereafter,

while they

with their Lord

set up equals.
