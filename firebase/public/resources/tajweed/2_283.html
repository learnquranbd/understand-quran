And if

you are

on

a journey

and not

you find

a scribe,

then pledge

in hand.

Then if

entrusts

one of you

(to) another

then let discharge

the one who

is entrusted

his trust.

And let him fear

Allah,

his Lord.

And (do) not

conceal

the evidence.

And whoever

conceals it,

then indeed he

(is) sinful -

his heart.

And Allah

of what

you do

(is) All-Knower.
