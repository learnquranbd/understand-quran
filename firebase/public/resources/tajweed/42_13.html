He has ordained

for you

of

the religion

what

He enjoined

upon

Nuh,

and that which

We have revealed

to you,

and what

We enjoined

upon

Ibrahim

and Musa

and Isa.

To

establish

the religion

and not

be divided

therein.

Is difficult

on

the polytheists

what

you call them

to it.

Allah

chooses

for Himself

whom

He wills,

and guides

to Himself

whoever

turns.
