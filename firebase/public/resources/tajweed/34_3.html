But say

those who

disbelieve,

"Not

will come to us

the Hour."

Say,

"Nay,

by my Lord

surely it will come to you.

(He is the) Knower

(of) the unseen."

Not

escapes

from Him

(the) weight

(of) an atom

in

the heavens

and not

in

the earth

and not

smaller

than

that

and not

greater,

but

(is) in

a Record

Clear.
