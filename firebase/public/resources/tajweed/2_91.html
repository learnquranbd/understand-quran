And when

it is said

to them,

"Believe

in what

has revealed

Allah,"

they say,

"We believe

in what

was revealed

to us."

And they disbelieve

in what

(is) besides it,

while it

(is) the truth

confirming

what

(is) with them.

Say,

"Then why

(did) you kill

(the) Prophets

(of) Allah

from

before,

if

you were

believers?"
