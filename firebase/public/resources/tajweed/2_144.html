Indeed,

We see

(the) turning

(of) your face

towards

the heaven.

So We will surely turn you

(to the) direction of prayer

you will be pleased with.

So turn

your face

towards the direction

(of) Al-Masjid

Al-Haraam

and wherever

that

you are

[so] turn

your faces

(in) its direction.

And indeed,

those who

were given

the Book

surely know

that it

(is) the truth

from

their Lord.

And not

(is) Allah

unaware

of what

they do.
