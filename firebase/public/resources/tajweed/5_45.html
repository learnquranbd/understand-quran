And We ordained

for them

in it

that -

the life

for the life,

and the eye

for the eye,

and the nose

for the nose,

and the ear

for the ear,

and the tooth

for the tooth,

and (for) wounds

(is) retribution.

But whoever

gives charity

with it,

then it is

an expiation

for him.

And whoever

(does) not

judge

by what

has revealed

Allah,

then those

[they]

(are) the wrongdoers.
