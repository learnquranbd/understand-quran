And said,

the Jews

and the Christians

"We (are)

(the) children

(of) Allah

and His beloved."

Say,

"Then why

(does He) punish you

for your sins?"

Nay,

you (are)

human beings

from among (those)

He created.

He forgives

[for] whom

He wills

and punishes

whom

He wills.

And for Allah

(is the) dominion

(of) the heavens

and the earth

and whatever

(is) between them,

and to Him

(is) the final return.
