Then when

have passed

the sacred months,

the sacred months,

then kill

the polytheists

wherever

you find them

and seize them

and besiege them

and sit (in wait)

for them

(at) every

place of ambush.

But if

they repent

and establish

the prayer

and give

the zakah

then leave

their way.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
