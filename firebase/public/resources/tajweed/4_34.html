[The] men

(are) protectors

of

the women

because

(has) bestowed

Allah

some of them

over

others

and because

they spend

from

their wealth.

So the righteous women

(are) obedient,

guarding

in the unseen

that which

(orders) them to guard

(by) Allah.

And those (from) whom

you fear

their ill-conduct

then advise them,

and forsake them

in

the bed

and [finally] strike them.

Then if

they obey you

then (do) not

seek

against them

a way.

Indeed,

Allah

is

Most High,

Most Great.
