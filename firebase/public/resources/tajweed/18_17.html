And you (might) have seen

the sun,

when

it rose,

inclining away

from

their cave

to

the right,

and when

it set,

passing away from them

to

the left

while they

(lay) in

the open space

thereof.

That

(was) from

(the) Signs

(of) Allah.

Whoever

Allah guides

Allah guides

and he

(is) the guided one,

and whoever

He lets go astray

then never

you will find

for him

a protector,

a guide.
