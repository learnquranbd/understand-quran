And to

Thamud

(We sent) their brother

Salih.

He said,

"O my people!

Worship

Allah,

not

for you

any

god

other than Him.

Verily

has come to you

a clear proof

from

your Lord,

This

(is) a she-camel

(of) Allah

(it is) for you

a Sign.

So you leave her

(to) eat

on

(the) earth

(of) Allah,

and (do) not

touch her

with harm,

lest seizes you

a punishment

painful."
