Say,

"We believed

in Allah

and what

(is) revealed

on us

and what

was revealed

on

Ibrahim,

and Ishmael,

and Isaac,

and Yaqub,

and the descendents

and what

was given

(to) Musa,

and Isa,

and the Prophets

from

their Lord.

Not

we make distinction

between

any

of them

and we

to Him

(are) submissive.
