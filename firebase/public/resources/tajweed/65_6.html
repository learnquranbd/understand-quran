Lodge them

from

where

you dwell,

(out) of

your means

and (do) not

harm them

to distress

[on] them.

And if

they are

those (who are)

pregnant,

then spend

on them

until

they deliver

their burden.

Then if

they suckle

for you,

then give them

their payment,

and consult

among yourselves

with kindness,

but if

you disagree,

then may suckle

for him

another (women).
