And when

asked (for) water

Musa

for his people,

[so] We said,

"Strike

with your staff

the stone."

Then gushed forth

from it

(of)

twelve

springs.

Indeed

knew

all

(the) people

their drinking place.

"Eat

and drink

from

(the) provision (of)

Allah,

and (do) not

act wickedly

in

the earth

spreading corruption."
