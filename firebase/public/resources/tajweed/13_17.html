He sends down

from

the sky

water

and flows

the valleys

according to their measure,

and carries

the torrent

a foam

rising.

And from what

they heat

[on] it

in

the fire

in order to make

ornaments

or

utensils,

a foam

like it.

Thus

sets forth

Allah

the truth

and the falsehood.

Then as for

the foam

it passes away

(as) scum,

and as for

what

benefits

the mankind,

remains

in

the earth.

Thus

Allah sets forth

Allah sets forth

the examples.
