(On the) Day

will say

the hypocrite men

and the hypocrite women

to those who

believed,

"Wait for us,

we may acquire

of

your light."

It will be said,

"Go back

behind you

and seek

light."

Then will be put up

between them

a wall,

for it

a gate

its interior,

in it

(is) mercy

but its exterior,

facing towards [it]

facing towards [it]

the punishment.
