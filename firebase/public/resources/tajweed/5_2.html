O

you (who)

believe!

(Do) not

violate

(the) rites

(of) Allah,

and not

the month,

the sacred,

and not

the sacrificial animals

and not

the garlanded

and not

(those) coming

(to) the House,

the Sacred,

seeking

Bounty

of

their Lord

and good pleasure.

And when

you come out of Ihram

then (you may) hunt.

And let not

incite you

(the) hatred

(for) a people

as

they stopped you

from

Al-Masjid

Al-Haraam

that

you commit transgression.

And help one another

in

[the] righteousness

and [the] piety,

but (do) not

help one another

in

[the] sin

and [the] transgression.

And fear

Allah;

indeed,

Allah

(is) severe

(in) [the] punishment.
