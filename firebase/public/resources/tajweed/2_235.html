And (there is) no

blame

upon you

in what

you hint

[with it]

of

marriage proposal

[to] the women

or

you conceal it

in

yourselves.

Knows

Allah

that you

will mention them,

[and] but

(do) not

promise them (widows)

secretly

except

that

you say

a saying

honorable.

And (do) not

resolve (on)

the knot

(of) marriage

until

reaches

the prescribed term

its end.

And know

that

Allah

knows

what

(is) within

yourselves

so beware of Him.

And know

that

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Forbearing.
