They said,

"Are you indeed,

surely you,

Yusuf?"

He said,

"I am

Yusuf

and this

(is) my brother.

Indeed,

Allah has been gracious

Allah has been gracious

to us.

Indeed, he

who

fears Allah

and (is) patient,

then indeed,

Allah

(does) not

let go waste

(the) reward

(of) the good-doers."
