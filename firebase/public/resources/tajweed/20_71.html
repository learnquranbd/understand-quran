He said,

"You believe

[to] him

before

[that]

I gave permission

to you.

Indeed, he

(is) your chief,

the one who

taught you

the magic.

So surely I will cut off

your hands

and your feet

of

opposite sides,

and surely I will crucify you

on

(the) trunks

(of) date-palms

and surely you will know

which of us

(is) more severe

(in) punishment

and more lasting."
