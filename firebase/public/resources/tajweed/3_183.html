Those who

said,

"Indeed

Allah

(has) taken promise

from us

that not

we (should) believe

in a Messenger

until

he brings to us

a sacrifice -

consumes it

the fire."

Say,

"Surely

came to you

Messengers

from

before me

with the clear Signs

and with what

you speak.

So why

you killed them,

if

you are

truthful.
