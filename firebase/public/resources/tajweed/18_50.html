And when

We said

to the Angels,

"Prostrate

to Adam,"

so they prostrated

except

Iblis.

(He) was

of

the jinn,

and he rebelled

against

the Command

(of) his Lord.

Will you then take him

and his offspring

(as) protectors

other than Me,

other than Me,

while they

(are) to you

enemies?

Wretched

for the wrongdoers

(is) the exchange.
