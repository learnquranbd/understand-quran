Said

their Messengers,

"Can (there) be about

Allah

any doubt,

(the) Creator

(of) the heavens

and the earth?

He invites you,

so that He may forgive

for you

[of]

your sins,

and give you respite

for

a term

appointed."

They said,

"Not

you

(are) but

a human

like us,

you wish

to

hinder us

from what

used to

worship

our forefathers.

So bring us

an authority

clear."
