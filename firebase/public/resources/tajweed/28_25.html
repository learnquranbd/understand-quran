Then came to him

one of the two women

walking

with

shyness.

She said,

"Indeed,

my father

calls you,

that he may reward you

(the) reward

(for) what

you watered

for us."

So when

he came to him

and narrated

to him

the story,

he said,

"(Do) not

fear.

You have escaped

from

the people -

the wrongdoers."
