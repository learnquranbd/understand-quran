Then if

they turn away,

then not

We have sent you

over them

(as) a guardian.

Not

(is) on you

except

the conveyance.

And indeed,

when

We cause to taste

[the] man

from Us

Mercy,

he rejoices

in it.

But if

befalls them

evil,

for what

have sent forth

their hands

then indeed,

[the] man

(is) ungrateful.
