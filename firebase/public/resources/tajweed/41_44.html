And if

We (had) made it

a Quran

(in) a foreign (language),

they (would have) said,

"Why not

are explained in detail

its verses?

(Is it) a foreign (language)

and an Arab?"

Say,

"It (is)

for those who

believe,

a guidance

and a healing."

And those who

(do) not

believe,

in

their ears

(is) deafness,

and it

(is) for them

blindness.

Those

are being called

from

a place

far."
