So for

their breaking

(of) their covenant

We cursed them

and We made

their hearts

hard.

They distort

the words

from

their places,

and forgot

a part

of what

they were reminded

of [it].

And not

will you cease

to discover

of

treachery

from them

except

a few

of them.

But forgive

them

and overlook.

Indeed,

Allah

loves

the good-doers.
