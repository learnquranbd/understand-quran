And said

the Jews,

"The Hand

(of) Allah

(is) chained."

Are chained

their hands,

and they have been cursed

for what

they said.

Nay,

His Hands

(are) stretched out

He spends

how

He wills.

And surely increase

many

of them,

what

has been revealed

to you

from

your Lord,

(in) rebellion

and disbelief.

And We have cast

among them

[the] enmity

and [the] hatred

till

(the) Day

(of) the Resurrection.

Every time

they kindled

(the) fire

of [the] war,

it (was) extinguished

(by) Allah.

And they strive

in

the earth

spreading corruption.

And Allah

(does) not

love

the corrupters.
