And be patient,

yourself,

with

those who

call

their Lord

in the morning

and the evening

desiring

His Face.

And (let) not

pass beyond

your eyes

over them,

desiring

adornment

(of) the life

(of) the world,

and (do) not

obey

whom

We have made heedless

his heart

of

Our remembrance,

and follows

his desires

and is

his affair

(in) excess.
