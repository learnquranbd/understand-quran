Say,

"Who

has forbidden

(the) adornment

(from) Allah

which

He has brought forth

for His slaves,

and the pure things

of

sustenance?"

Say,

"They

(are) for those who

believe

during

the life

(of) the world,

exclusively (for them)

(on the) Day

(of) Resurrection.

Thus

We explain

the Signs

for (the) people

who know."
