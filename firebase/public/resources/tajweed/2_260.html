And when

said

Ibrahim,

"My Lord

show me

how

You give life

(to) the dead."

He said,

"Have not

you believed?"

He said,

"Yes

[and] but

to satisfy

my heart."

He said

"Then take

four

of

the birds

and incline them

towards you,

then

put

on

each

hill

of them

a portion;

then

call them,

they will come to you

(in) haste.

And know

that

Allah

(is) All-Mighty,

All-Wise.
