Allah (has) made

Allah (has) made

for any man

[of]

two hearts

in

his interior.

your wives

whom

you declare unlawful

[of them]

And not

He has made

your adopted sons

your sons.

(is) your saying

by your mouths,

but Allah

says

the truth,

and He

guides
