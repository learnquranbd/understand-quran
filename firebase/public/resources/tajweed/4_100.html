And whoever

emigrates

in

(the) way

(of) Allah,

will find

in

the earth

place(s) of refuge -

many,

and abundance.

And whoever

leaves

from

his home

(as) an emigrant

to

Allah

and His Messenger,

then

overtakes him

[the] death,

then certainly

(became) incumbent

his reward

on

Allah.

And is

Allah

Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
