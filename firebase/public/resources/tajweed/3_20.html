Then if

they argue with you,

then say,

"I have submitted

myself

to Allah

and (those) who

follow me."

And say

to those who

were given

the Book,

and the unlettered people,

"Have you submitted yourselves?"

Then if

they submit

then surely

they are guided.

But if

they turn back

then only

on you

(is) to [the] convey.

And Allah

(is) All-Seer

of [His] slaves.
