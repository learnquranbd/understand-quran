Indeed,

Allah

(has) purchased

from

the believers

their lives

and their wealth,

because

for them

(is) Paradise.

They fight

in

(the) way

(of) Allah,

they slay

and they are slain.

A promise

upon Him

true,

in

the Taurat

and the Injeel

and the Quran.

And who

(is) more faithful

to his promise

than

Allah?

So rejoice

in your transaction

which

you have contracted

[with it].

And that

it

(is) the success

the great.
