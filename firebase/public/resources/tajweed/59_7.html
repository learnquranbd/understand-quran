What

(was) restored

(by) Allah

to

His Messenger

from

(the) people

(of) the towns,

(it is) for Allah

and His Messenger

and for those

(of) the kindred

and the orphans

and the needy

and

the wayfarer,

that

not

it becomes

a (perpetual) circulation

between

the rich

among you.

And whatever

gives you

the Messenger,

take it

and whatever

he forbids you

from it,

refrain.

And fear

Allah.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) severe

(in) penalty.
