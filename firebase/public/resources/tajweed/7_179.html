And certainly

We have created

for Hell

many

of

the jinn

and men.

For them

(are) hearts

(but) not

they understand

with them,

and for them

(are) eyes

(but) not

they see

with them,

and for them

(are) ears

(but) not

they hear

with them.

Those

(are) like cattle,

nay

they

(are) more astray.

Those -

they

(are) the heedless.
