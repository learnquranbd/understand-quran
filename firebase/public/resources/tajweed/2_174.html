Indeed,

those who

conceal

what

(has) revealed

Allah (has)

of

the Book,

and they purchase

there with

a gain

little.

not

they eat

in

their bellies

except

the Fire.

will speak to them

Allah

(on the) Day

(of) [the] Judgment

and not

painful.
