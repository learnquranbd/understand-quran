O you

who

believe!

When

come to you

the believing women

(as) emigrants,

then examine them.

Allah

(is) most knowing

of their faith.

And if

you know them

(to be) believers,

then (do) not

return them

to

the disbelievers.

Not

they

(are) lawful

for them

and not

they

are lawful

for them.

But give them

what

they have spent.

And not

any blame

upon you

if

you marry them

when

you have given them

their (bridal) dues.

And (do) not

hold

to marriage bonds

(with) disbelieving women,

but ask (for)

what

you have spent,

and let them ask

what

they have spent.

That

(is the) Judgment

(of) Allah.

He judges

between you.

And Allah

(is) All-Knowing,

All-Wise.
