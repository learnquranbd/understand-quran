Say,

"Shall

I inform you

(of) worse

than

that

(as) recompense

from

Allah?

Whom

has (been) cursed

(by) Allah

and He became angry

with him

and made

of them

[the] apes

and [the] swines,

and (who) worshipped

the false deities.

Those

(are) worse

(in) position

and farthest astray

from

(the) even

way."
