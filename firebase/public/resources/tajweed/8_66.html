Now

has (been) lightened

(by) Allah

for you,

and He knows

that

in you

(there) is weakness.

So if

(there) are

among you

a hundred

steadfast,

they will overcome

two hundred.

And if

(there) are

among you

a thousand,

they will overcome

two thousand

with (the) permission

(of) Allah.

And Allah

(is) with

the steadfast.
