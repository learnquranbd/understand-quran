And leave

those who

take

their religion

(as) a play

and amusement

and deluded them

the life

(of) the world.

But remind

with it,

lest

is given up to destruction

a soul

for what

it (has) earned,

not

(is) for it

from

besides

Allah

any protector

and not

any intercessor.

And if

it offers ransom -

every

ransom,

not

will it be taken

from it.

Those

(are) ones who

are given to destruction

for what

they earned.

For them

(will be) a drink

of

boiling water

and a punishment

painful

because

they used to

disbelieve.
