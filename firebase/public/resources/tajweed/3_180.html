And (let) not

think

those who

withhold

of what

(has) given them

Allah

of

His Bounty

(that) it

(is) good

for them.

Nay,

it

(is) bad

for them.

Their necks will be encircled

(with) what

they withheld

[with it]

(on the) Day

(of) [the] Resurrection.

And for Allah

(is the) heritage

(of) the heavens

and the earth.

And Allah,

with what

you do,

(is) All-Aware.
