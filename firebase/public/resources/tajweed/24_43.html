Do not

you see

that

Allah

drives

clouds

then

joins

between them

then

makes them

(into) a mass,

then you see

the rain

come forth

from

their midst?

And He sends down

from

(the) sky,

[from]

mountains

within it

[of]

(is) hail

and He strikes

with it

whom

He wills

and averts it

from

whom

He wills.

Nearly

(the) flash

(of) its lighting

takes away

the sight.
