Then is (he) who -

is made fair-seeming

to him

(the) evil

(of) his deed -

so that he sees it

(as) good?

For indeed,

Allah

lets go astray

whom

He wills

and guides

whom

He wills.

So (let) not

go out

your soul

for them

(in) regrets.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) All-Knower

of what

they do.
