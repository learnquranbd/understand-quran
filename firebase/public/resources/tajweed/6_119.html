And what

for you

that not

you eat

of what

has been mentioned

(the) name

(of) Allah

on it,

when indeed,

He (has) explained in detail

to you

what

He (has) forbidden

to you

except

what

you are compelled

to it.

And indeed,

many

surely lead astray

by their (vain) desires

without

knowledge.

Indeed,

your Lord,

He

(is) most knowing

of the transgressors.
