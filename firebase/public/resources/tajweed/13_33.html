Is then He Who

(He)

(is) a Maintainer

of

every

soul

for what

it has earned?

Yet they ascribe

to Allah

partners.

Say,

"Name them.

Or

(do) you inform Him

of what

not

He knows

in

the earth

or

of the apparent

of

the words?"

Nay,

(is) made fair-seeming

to those who

disbelieve

their plotting,

and they are hindered

from

the Path.

And whoever

(by) Allah

Allah lets go astray

then not

for him

any

guide.
