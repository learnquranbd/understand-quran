"O Dawood!

Indeed, We

[We] have made you

a vicegerent

in

the earth,

so judge

between

[the] men

in truth

and (do) not

follow

the desire,

for it will lead you astray

from

(the) way

(of) Allah.

Indeed,

those who

go astray

from

(the) way

(of) Allah,

for them

(is) a punishment

severe

because

they forgot

(the) Day

(of) Account."
