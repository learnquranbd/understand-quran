And test

the orphans

until

[when]

they reach[ed]

(the age of) marriage,

then if

you perceive

in them

sound judgement

then deliver

to them

their wealth.

And (do) not

eat it

extravagantly

and hastily

(fearing) that

they will grow up.

And whoever

is

rich

then he should refrain,

and whoever

is

poor

then let him eat (of it)

in a fair manner.

Then when

you deliver

to them

their wealth

then take witnesses

on them.

And is sufficient

Allah

(as) a Reckoner.
