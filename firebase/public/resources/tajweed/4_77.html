Have not

you seen

[towards]

those who

(when) it was said

to them,

"Restrain

your hands

and establish

the prayer

and give

the zakah?"

Then when

was ordained

on them

the fighting,

then

a group

of them

[they] fear

the people

as (they) fear

Allah

or

more intense

fear,

and they said,

"Our Lord

why

have You ordained

upon us

[the] fighting?

Why not

You postpone (it for) us

to

a term,

near."

Say,

"Enjoyment

(of) the world

(is) little

and the Hereafter

(is) better

for whoever

fears (Allah)

and not

you will be wronged

(even as much as) a hair on a date-seed."
