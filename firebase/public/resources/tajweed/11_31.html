And not

I say

to you

(that) with me

(are the) treasures

(of) Allah,

and not

I know

the unseen,

and not

I say

that I am

an Angel,

and not

I say

for those whom

look down upon

your eyes,

never

will Allah give them

will Allah give them

any good.

Allah

knows best

what

(is) in

their souls.

Indeed, I

then

(will be) surely of

the wrongdoers."
