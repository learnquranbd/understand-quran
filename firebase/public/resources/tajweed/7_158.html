Say,

"O

mankind!

Indeed I am

(the) Messenger

(of) Allah

to you

all,

the One

for Whom

(is the) dominion

(of) the heavens

and the earth.

(There is) no

god

except

Him,

He gives life

and causes death.

So believe

in Allah

and His Messenger,

the Prophet,

the unlettered

the one who

believes

in Allah

and His Words,

and follow him

so that you may

(be) guided."
