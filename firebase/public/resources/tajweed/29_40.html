So each

We seized

for his sin.

Then of them

(was he) who,

We sent

on him

a violent storm,

and of them

(was he) who,

seized him

the awful cry

and of them

(was he) who,

We caused to swallow

him,

the earth

and of them

(was he) who,

We drowned.

And not

was

Allah

to wrong them

but

they were

themselves

doing wrong.
