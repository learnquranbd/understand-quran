And who

(is) more wrong

than (he) who

is reminded

of the Verses

(of) his Lord,

but turns away

from them,

and forgets

what

have sent forth

his hands?

Indeed, We

[We] have placed

over

their hearts

coverings,

lest

they understand it

and in

their ears

(is) deafness.

And if

you call them

to

the guidance,

then never

they will be guided

then

ever.
