For the poor,

those who

are wrapped up

in

(the) way

(of) Allah,

not

they are able

(to) move about

in

the earth.

Think (about) them,

the ignorant one,

(that they are) self-sufficient

(because) of

(their) restraint,

you recognize them

by their mark.

Not

(do) they ask

the people

with importunity.

And whatever

you spend

of

good,

then indeed,

Allah

of it

(is) All-Knower.
