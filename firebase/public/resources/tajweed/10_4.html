To Him,

(will be) your return

[all].

Promise

(of) Allah

(is) true.

Indeed, He

originates

the creation,

then

He repeats it,

that He may reward

those who

believed

and did

the good deeds,

in justice.

But those who

disbelieved,

for them

(will be) a drink

of

boiling fluids

and a punishment

painful,

because

they used (to)

disbelieve.
