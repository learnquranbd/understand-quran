Indeed,

those who

exchange

(the) Covenant

(of) Allah

and their oaths

(for) a price

little,

those -

no

share

for them

in

the Hereafter

and not

will speak to them

Allah,

and not

look

at them

(on the) Day

(of) the Resurrection

and not

purify them,

and for them

(is) a punishment

painful.
