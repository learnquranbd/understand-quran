And from

wherever

you start forth

[so] turn

your face

(in the) direction

(of) Al-Masjid

Al-Haraam.

And wherever

that

you (all) are

[so] turn

your faces

(in) its direction,

so that not

will be

for the people

against you

any argument

except

those who

wronged

among them;

so (do) not

fear them,

but fear Me.

And that I complete

My favor

upon you

[and] so that you may

(be) guided.
