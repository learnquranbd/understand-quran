A Messenger

reciting

to you

(the) Verses

(of) Allah

clear,

that he may bring out

those who

believe

and do

righteous deeds

from

the darkness[es]

towards

the light.

And whoever

believes

in Allah

and does

righteous deeds,

He will admit him

(into) Gardens

flow

from

underneath it

the rivers,

abiding

therein

forever.

Indeed,

Has been (granted) good

(by) Allah

for him

provision.
