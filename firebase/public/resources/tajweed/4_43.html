O you

who

believe[d]!

(Do) not

go near

the prayer

while you

(are) intoxicated

until

you know

what

you are saying

and not

(when you are) impure

except

(when) passing

(through) a way

until

you have bathed.

And if

you are

ill

or

on

a journey

or

came

one

of you

from

the toilet,

or

you have touched

the women

and not

you find

water,

then do tayammum

(with) earth

clean

and wipe (with it)

your faces

and your hands.

Indeed,

Allah

is

Oft-Pardoning,

Oft-Forgiving.
