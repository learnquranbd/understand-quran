which

is promised

(to) the righteous.

(are) rivers

of

water

not

polluted,

and rivers

of

milk

not

changes

its taste,

and rivers

of

wine

delicious

for (the) drinkers,

and rivers

of

honey

all

fruits

their Lord

like he who
