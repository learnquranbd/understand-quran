And (prohibited are) the ones who are married

of

the women

except

whom

you possess

rightfully.

Decree

(of) Allah

upon you.

And are lawful

to you

what

(is) beyond

that;

that

you seek

with your wealth

desiring to be chaste

not

(to be) lustful.

So what

you benefit[ed]

of it

from them,

so you give them

their bridal due

(as) an obligation.

And (there is) no

sin

on you

concerning what

you mutually agree

of it

from

beyond

the obligation.

Indeed,

Allah

is

All-Knowing,

All-Wise.
