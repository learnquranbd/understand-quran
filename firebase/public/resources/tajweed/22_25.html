Indeed,

those who

disbelieved

and hinder

from

(the) way

(of) Allah

and Al-Masjid Al-Haraam,

and Al-Masjid Al-Haraam,

which

We made it

for the mankind,

equal,

(are) the resident

therein

and the visitor;

and whoever

intends

therein

of deviation

(or) wrongdoing,

We will make him taste

of

a punishment

painful.
