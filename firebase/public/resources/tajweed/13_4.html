And in

the earth

are tracks

neighboring,

and gardens

of

grapevines

and crops

and date-palms

trees (growing) from a single root

and not

trees (growing) from a single root.

watered

with water,

one;

but We cause to exceed

some of them

over

others

in

the fruit.

Indeed,

in

that

surely (are) Signs

for a people

who use reason.
