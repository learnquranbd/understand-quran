Here you are -

these,

called

to spend

in

(the) way

(of) Allah -

but among you

(are some) who

withhold,

and whoever

withholds,

then only

he withholds

from

himself.

But Allah

(is) Free of need,

while you

(are) the needy.

And if

you turn away

He will replace you

(with) a people

other than you,

then

not

they will be

(the) likes of you.
