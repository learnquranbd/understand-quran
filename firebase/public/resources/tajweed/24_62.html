Only

the believers

(are) those who

believe

in Allah

and His Messenger,

and when

they are

with him

for

a matter

(of) collective action,

not

they go

until

they (have) asked his permission.

Indeed,

those who

ask your permission,

those

[those who]

believe

in Allah

and His Messenger.

So when

they ask your permission

for some

affair of theirs,

then give permission

to whom

you will

among them,

and ask forgiveness

for them

(of) Allah.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
