Only

(the) example

(of) the life

(of) the world

(is) like (the) water

which We sent down

from

the sky,

so absorbs

[with] it,

(the) plants

(of) the earth

from which

eat

the men

and the cattle,

until

when

takes

the earth

its adornment

and is beautified

and think

its people

that they

have the power

over it,

comes (to) it

Our command

(by) night

or

(by) day,

and We make it

a harvest clean-mown,

as if

not

it had flourished

yesterday.

Thus

We explain

the Signs

for a people

who reflect.
