So they defeated them

by (the) permission

(of) Allah,

and killed

Dawood

Jalut,

and gave him

Allah

the kingdom

and the wisdom

and taught him

that which

He willed.

And if not

(for the) repelling

(by) Allah,

[the] people -

some of them

with others,

certainly (would have) corrupted

the Earth,

[and] but

Allah

(is) Possessor

(of) bounty

to

the worlds.
