O

Prophet!

When

come to you

the believing women

pledging to you

[on]

that

not

they will associate

with Allah

anything,

and not

they will steal,

and not

they will commit adultery,

and not

they will kill

their children,

and not

they bring

slander,

they invent it

between

their hands

and their feet,

and not

they will disobey you

in

(the) right,

then accept their pledge

and ask forgiveness

for them

(from) Allah.

Indeed,

Allah

(is) Oft-Forgiving,

Most Merciful.
