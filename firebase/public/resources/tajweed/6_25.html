And among them

(are those) who

listen

to you,

but We have placed

over

their hearts

coverings

lest

they understand it,

and in

their ears

deafness.

And if

they see

every

sign

not

will they believe

in it.

Until,

when

they come to you

and argue with you

say

those who

disbelieved,

"Not

(is) this

but

(the) tales

(of) the former (people)."
