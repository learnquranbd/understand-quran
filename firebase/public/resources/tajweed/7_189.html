He

(is) the One Who

created you

from

a soul,

single

and made

from it

its mate

that he might live

with her.

And when

he covers her,

she carries

a burden

light

and continues

with it.

But when

she grows heavy,

they both invoke

Allah,

their Lord,

"If

You give us

a righteous (child),

surely we will be

among

the thankful."
