And give good news

(to) those who

believe,

and do

[the] righteous deeds,

that

for them

(will be) Gardens,

flow

[from]

under them

the rivers.

Every time

they are provided

therefrom

of

fruit

(as) provision,

they (will) say,

"This (is)

the one which

we were provided

from

before."

And they will be given

therefrom

(things) in resemblance;

And for them

therein

spouses

purified,

and they

therein

(will) abide forever.
