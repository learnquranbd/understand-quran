And to Sulaiman,

the wind -

its morning course

(was) a month

and its afternoon course

(was) a month,

and We caused to flow

for him

a spring

(of) molten copper.

And [of]

the jinn

who

worked

before him

before him

by the permission

(of) his Lord.

And whoever

deviated

among them

from

Our Command,

We will make him taste

of

(the) punishment

(of) the Blaze.
