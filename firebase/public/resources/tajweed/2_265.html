And (the) example

(of) those who

spend

their wealth

seeking

(the) pleasure

(of) Allah,

and certainty

from

their (inner) souls,

(is) like

a garden

on a height,

fell on it

heavy rain

so it yielded

its harvest

double.

Then if

(does) not

fall (on) it

heavy rain,

then a drizzle.

And Allah

of what

you do

(is) All-Seer.
