(Do) not

make

(the) calling

(of) the Messenger

among you

as (the) call

(of) some of you

(to) others.

Verily,

Allah knows

Allah knows

those who

slip away

among you

under shelter.

So let beware

those who

oppose

[from]

his orders

lest

befalls them

a trial

or

befalls them

a punishment

painful.
