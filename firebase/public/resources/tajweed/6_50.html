Say,

"Not

(do) I say

to you

(that) with me

(are the) treasures

(of) Allah

and not

(that) I know

the unseen

and not

I say

to you

that I (am)

an Angel.

Not

(do) I follow

except

what

is revealed

to me."

Say,

"Can

be equal

the blind

and the seeing one?"

Then will not

you give thought?
